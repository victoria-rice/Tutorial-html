Han Shot First

George Lucas thought the scene between Han Solo ad Greedo in the Cantina made Han cold blooded. Lets think about the original scene, shall we? Greedo the bounty hunteris holding Han at gunpoint and says in his gurgly voice he is going to kill him. Han shoots Greedo through the table before Greedo can blow Han a new smuggling hatch. That makes Han cool, not cold.
